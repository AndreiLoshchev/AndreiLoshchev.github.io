KAI Ad Fraud Prevention

KAI is an AI-driven fraud prevention technology with ability to detect and block more than 20 distinct fraud categories across a wide spectrum of programmatic media avenues, inventory classifications, and advertisement formats/placements. Machine Learning solution based on Unsupervised approach was able to detect various sophisticated fraud like Synthnet and Weasel fraud.

NCSR-parsing

NCSR parsing's primary goal was to empower precise predictions across 8 distinct classes of tables within the complex landscape of HTML SEC N-CSR form reports. Through strategic modifications and the adoption of the XGBoost multi-classifier, I achieved remarkable strides in enhancing the accuracy of our predictive models to detect various financial tables across of 1000 reports from renowned institutions such as Vanguard, Blackrock, iShares, Wells Fargo, and others..

EveaSale Demo

The web app is aimed to weekly forecast and predict the future sales and make business-oriented analysis. The client had more than 3 years historical POS-data of more than 100 branch of a chain restaurant. For more information check out the EveaSale website and project report.
